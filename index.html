2. Sources of Conflict
(15 min)

‚ùÆ
Discrimination

Includes untoward comments based on false biases. Reasons for discimination include: race, gender, disability, physical appearance, language, and ethnicity or culture. It can also include nationality or immigration status.

Groups covered by Employment Equity Act (1995)
  • Women
  • Aboriginal peoples
  • Persons with disabilities
  • Members of visible minorities
Language Barrier

Limitations of Official Languages Act (1984):

  • Official workplace languages can only be English or French.
  • Enforcement follows provincial law (e.g., BC vs. Quebec).

Misunderstanding due to Language

Occur when a cohesive language is not present in meetings. Some languages are also tonal and can sound aggressive or create misconceptions of anger to non-speakers.

Communication Techniques

Nonviolent Communication (NVC)

  1. Observe (no judgments)
  2. Share feeling (‚ÄúI felt‚Ä¶‚Äù)
  3. Name need (e.g., clarity, respect)
  4. Request (concrete, doable)

SBI Framework

  1. Situation: When/where
  2. Behavior: What you did/said
  3. Impact: How it affected me/others
